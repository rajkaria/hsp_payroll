HASHPAY / PROTOCOL
02
what we built

Not a product.
A protocol.

Six composable on-chain primitives. Every read is permissionless. Anyone — any team, any protocol, any chain — can compose on top.

01 · how money flows Cadence recipient’s choice Batch, stream, pull, or hybrid split. One payroll, every cadence. AdaptiveCadence.sol
02 · what idle money does Yield runway extends itself ERC-4626 auto-deposit. 6-month runway becomes 7.2 months. Zero ops change. YieldEscrow.sol
03 · what receipts compose into Reputation proof-of-income, permissionless Every attestation aggregates. Chainlink-compatible oracle. Any protocol reads it. ReputationRegistry.sol
04 · receipt-backed credit Advances the PayFi loop, closed Borrow up to 70% of next payout. Reputation-tiered APR. Auto-repaid on cycle execute. PayrollAdvance.sol
05 · pluggable gating Compliance as composition, not code KYC, jurisdiction, sanctions, rate limits, timelocks. Configurable per-payroll. ComplianceHookRegistry.sol
06 · fiat-denominated pay Salary Index built for emerging markets Quote in INR, USD, EUR. Chainlink oracle resolves to tokens at execute time. SalaryIndex.sol
